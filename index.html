Diamond space collector

Rules

  • In the beginning of the game your health is 6 points.
  • You have to collect diamonds in 30 points to protect the ships and win.
  • Every diamond gives you 5 points.
  • Be careful, you can get a diamond fixture in front of the ship.
  • If you bump into an enemy ship, you lose 3 points.
  • Your health is zero you lose.
  • You have to control the ship arrows on the keyboard.
